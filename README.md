# Chinese New Year 春節 — Community Archive & Advocacy

A community-driven website that:

1. **Documents** how companies and institutions refer to Chinese New Year
2. **Explains** why the historically accurate name "Chinese New Year" (aka Spring Festival, 春節) matters

🌐 **Live site:** [chinesenewyear.wiki](https://chinesenewyear.wiki/)

---

## Why This Project?

The term **Chinese New Year** (春節) carries thousands of years of cultural heritage rooted in the Chinese calendar, Chinese cosmology, and Chinese astronomical tradition. Recently, corporations and institutions have been rebranding it as "Lunar New Year" — a term that is:

- **Scientifically inaccurate** (this date is the new year date on "農曆", the Chinese Calendar, which is NOT a lunar calendar). It is determined scientifically based on the MERIDIAN LINE and TIMEZONE of China)
- **Historically rooted in colonial erasure** (originating from British colonial governance in Hong Kong in 1968)
- **Culturally disrespectful** (it culturally appropriates the real lunar new year used by Islamic communities, and purposefully removes Chinese origin in the name of "inclusion")
- **Contradictory in practice** (institutions rebrand the holiday as "Lunar New Year" to target broader audiences yet continue to use almost exclusively Chinese cultural elements — such as its red lanterns, 12 zodiac animals, fireworks & firecrackers, red envelopes, dragon & lion dance, etc. — in their promotional materials and advertisements, undermining their own claim of inclusivity)

This project advocates for preserving the correct name and educating people about why it matters.

---

## Features

- **Archive** — Side-by-side documentation of companies and institutions correctly use "Chinese New Year" vs. those using the problematic "Lunar New Year"
- **Educational Article** — A well-sourced article explaining the history, science, and cultural significance behind the name, available in English and Traditional Chinese (繁體中文)

### 为什么要做这个项目？

**中国农历新年**（春节）承载着数千年的文化传承，植根于中国历法、中国宇宙观与中国天文学传统，全世界也一直称之为「Chinese New Year」。然而近年来，许多企业和机构以'包容性'为名，将其宣传为「Lunar New Year」。这个名称存在诸多问题，且十分具有误导性，因为它：

- **在科学上不准确**（这一日期是「农历」上的新年日期，而农历并非纯阴历，而是阴阳合历。其日期是根据中国的子午线和时区通过科学计算确定的）
- **历史上根源于殖民抹杀**（源于1968年英国在香港的殖民管治）
- **在文化上不尊重**（它将这一日期与伊斯兰社区真正使用的纯阴历新年混为一谈，并以「包容」之名故意抹去中国文化起源）
- **实践上前后矛盾**（机构一边将节日重新品牌化为「Lunar New Year」以覆盖更广泛的受众，一边在宣传材料和广告中几乎清一色使用中国文化元素 —— 如红灯笼、十二生肖、烟花爆竹、红包、舞龙舞狮等等 —— 非常的自我矛盾）

本项目致力于维护中国农历新年（春节）正确的名称，并向公众普及其背后的重要意义。
